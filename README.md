# LUGAN | Linux User Group Ancona üêß

A **Linux User Group (LUG)** is a social group formed by people who uphold the principles of **Free Software** and are committed to spreading the use of operating systems based on the Linux kernel. **LUGAN** extends this foundation with a core focus on **research, active development, and social impact**. We are a community of researchers, developers, and enthusiasts dedicated to **building, not just using**.

## üìç We are in Ancona

We are based in **Ancona**, the capital city of the Marche region (Italy), renowned for its historic port, the blue waters of Portonovo and the scenic hills of Monte Conero; home to the **Marche Polytechnic University (UNIVPM)**.
